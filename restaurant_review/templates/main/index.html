Download the Soma Ko mobile application

Our services are carried out with the help of our online platform; which is a mobile application and a web application. We also make use of Offline messaging such as SMS, MMS and simple Phone calls.

After booking for your errand, clients can track the location of the errand and the direction of the errands on the app through an integrated live map on the client dashboard.
Clients also have the opportunity to mark the errand as completed upon delivery of the errands after crosschecking and making sure the errands was as planned.
Client can also send their review based on the performance of the errands. All errands can be tracked on the client dashboard.
